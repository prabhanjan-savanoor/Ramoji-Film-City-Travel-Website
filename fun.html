Fun things to do at Ramoji

Ramoji Film City is India's only thematic holiday destination with cine-magic. Certified as the World's Largest Film Studio complex by Guinness World Records, it spreads across 2000 acres. Millions of tourists visit the amusement park to live their dream vacation.Here are some among the many places that you should not miss out on-

Movie Magic Park

If you're a film lover, you this is a must try destination.You get to travel to different continents and visit Action Studio to get the feel of action-packed thrills on experiences like earthquake tremors, acoustic effects, and free falls.The sets of Ali Baba Aur Chalis Chor a artificial cities of Bangkok & Pattaya are some of the highlights of Movie Magic Park during your Ramoji Film City tour.


Fundustan

If you think Ramoji Film City would be boring for your kids, then you're wrond. Kids Park had multiple activities and rides,like Ballerina Fount, Timberland, Enthraller, Thrillville Rides, Wonderville, Borasura & many others.


Wings- TBird Park

Film City feels incomplete without a visit to this birds’ paradise.There are some many exotic bird species you can expect to spot here.You will be mesmerized by the stunning green spectacle accompanied by the melodious chirping of birds & the beautiful waterfalls in the background.


Eureka

The Eureka lets you take a stroll back into history to experience the age of the Mughals, the times when Mauryans ruled, and amazing national and international artefacts on your visit here.

Live Shows

Dome Theatre Shows, Spirit of Ramoji, a variety of Street shows, Wild West Stunt Shows, and opening & closing ceremonies are some of the live theatre Shows that you get to see here. Even if you aren’t a fan of theatre,it is recommend that you make a special note of these live shows.

Ramoji Film City is also a shoppers' paradise, where you can find many interesting things to take back as souvenirs.Here you have delectable food to savour and plenty of places to sit in, including Alampana Superstar, Gun-smoke, and Chanakya, among others.
